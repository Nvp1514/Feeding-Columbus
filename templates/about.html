Our mission is to gather food and keep people from being hungry.

Food is a necessity in life that everyone deserves access to. We strive to keep people fed while still providing nutritional value.

Read our story

Who We are

Feeding Columbus is a non-profit organization that's dedicated to keep everyone fed in the Chattahoochee Valley. We work with a large cast of people ranging from volunteers, local churches, and other food agencies. All food that is gathered is stored in one of our warehouses where we keep inventory and inspect before distrubuting to the community.

Growth & beyond

Our supporters continue to help our mission grow. We will continue to help feed the Chattahooche Valley for the foreseeable future to combat the hunger. With the help of our supporters, we aim to create a community where hunger is no longer a concern.

Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with our mission of combating hunger. Whether it's donating food, hosting food drives, or giving your time to assist, your contribution to our mission helps ensure that we live in a fed community.
