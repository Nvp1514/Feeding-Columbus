Together, We Can Combat Hunger

We strive to keep our community in the chattahoochee Valley Fed. No one deserves to go without a necessity in life

Learn More

Services that assist in the fight:

Emergency Food Distribution

In the case of any emergency situation, we offer distribution services to anyone that may be in need.

Pantry Services

We offer community spaces for the people in need that allows them access to both food and personal hygiene products.

Nutrition Education

We provide educational programs that help teach everyone on proper nutrition with the likes of workshops and recipes.

Meal Programs

We regularly run meal programs such as soup kitchens to allow people to recieve hot meals no matter the situation they find themselves in.
